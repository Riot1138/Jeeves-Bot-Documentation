# Mute Command

Quickly mute all the chatterboxes in the channel with you.
***
### Details
**Aliases:** `mute`   
**Available in DM:** No   
**Can Restrict to a channel:** Yes
***
### Examples

* `!mute`
>  Mutes all non-admins in the voice channel

* `!unmute`
>  Unmutes everyone in the voice channel
***
